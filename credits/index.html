Questions

The original sources of the questions and descriptions used for this quiz are the following Pokémon Mystery Dungeon (PMD) games:

  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Red/Blue Rescue Team
  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time/Darkness
  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky

Modifications from the Source

Minor modifications were made in a very limited number of cases.

  • A "Neither" option was added to the final "gender question" of the quiz.
  • The Hasty nature's description from PMD: Rescue Team was changed to reference the "Next" button rather than the "A" button.
  • Certain questions from PMD: Explorers of Sky are almost identical to questions from PMD: Explorers of Time/Darkness, up to minor phrasing differences. This quiz keeps phrasing consistent between games. Which of the two variants were used varies by question, depending on which I liked better.
